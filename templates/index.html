The International Day of Clean Air for Blue Skies

#CleanAirForBlueSkies

Did you know?

  • Air pollution is India's 2nd largest problem after malnutrition.
  • It is a pan-India problem, with over 100 cities in India designated as Non- Attainment cities.
  • Air pollution accounts for an estimated 12.5% of deaths in India.
  • Inhaling air pollution decreases the average human lifespan by 1 to 2 years.
  • Beijing's air pollution has given rise to a new disease, called the Beijing cough.

It's time we brought change.
